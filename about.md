I’m Sathvik.

This is a small blog where I write down things I learn so I don’t forget them.

Most of this will be:
- things I misunderstood at first  
- bugs I ran into
- small concepts that finally clicked  

---

### Why this exists

I realized I forget things way too easily, even things I’ve already learned.

So instead of relying on memory, I’m writing things down here.

---

### What to expect

Short posts.  
Sometimes wrong at first.  
Usually written right after I figure something out.  


These are notes written in the moment, not summaries or generated explanations.
